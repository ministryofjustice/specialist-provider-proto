How we calculated whether your client is financially eligible for civil controlled legal aid work

This calculation was made using the rules for controlled work in The Civil Legal Aid (Financial Resources and Payment for Services) Regulations 2013 (opens in new tab) and the amendments to these Regulations (opens in new tab).

Guidance on determining financial eligibility for controlled work and family mediation can be found in the guide to determining controlled work (PDF, 940KB).
